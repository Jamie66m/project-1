Hogwarts Invaders

Instructions

X

Harry beware as Voldemort has managed to take control of the dementors and release them from Azkaban. The protection of Hogwarts now lies with you.

Difficulty Levels:

Easy: 3 rows of dementors - dementor speed is slow - to defeat Voldemort takes 3 hits

Medium: 4 rows of dementors - dementor speed is normal - to defeat Voldemort takes 5 hits

Hard: 4 rows of dementors - dementor speed is slow - to defeat Voldemort takes 7 hits

Controls:

Move Harry: Left = Arrow Left, Right = Arrow Right

Cast Spell: Spacebar = Expecto Patronum, E Key = Expelliarmus
